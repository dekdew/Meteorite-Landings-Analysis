Meteorite Landings Analysis

The Meteoritical Society collects data on meteorites that have fallen to Earth from outer space. This dataset includes the location, mass, composition, and fall year for over 45,000 meteorites that have struck our planet.

From graph, the factor that made we found the meteorite's flasks is meteorite's mass and atmosphere. If the meteorites have enough mass, it can penetrate from atmosphere to Earth surface and exploed to many meteorite's flasks in area. In the case of the meteorites's mass that not enough to penetrate in atmosphere, It wil burn by friction force in atmosphere before it fall to the surface

From graph, we can assumed in two case. The technology advancement and the geological survey. In the technology case, by an advancement of technoogy can made sciencetist found many meteorites in our Earth in the past to present, using teloscrope, the power of magnetism and age of meteorites from biological verification. For unknown case, is from geological survey by biological verification or chemical verification with the meteorites in the layer and can't finded out the age of it, this proceses is similar to fossil verification or layer verification.
